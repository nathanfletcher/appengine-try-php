WHAT WE DO

THE FIRM

Awere-Kyere & Associates Ltd is a wholly Ghanaian-owned civil engineering consulting firm registered under the Ghana Companies Code 1963 (Act. 176). Awere-Kyere & Associates was set up to provide professional consulting engineering services to the construction industry.

The company has acquired experience in diverse civil engineering fields of endeavor by contributing in various ways to different projects.

These include undertaking feasibility studies, pre contract engineering studies, detailed engineering design, and supervision of construction works and management of engineering projects.

Awere-Kyere & Associates interacts closely with clients to define their needs. Using creativity, modern technology, computerized management systems and team working, we can achieve the best solution while seeking out economy, quality enhancement and safety in the accomplishment of specific tasks.

The firm's Head Office is located at Suite No. A4 Labone Court Yard, Accra. Awere-Kyere & Associates is capable of quickly mobilizing to undertake projects at different locations in Ghana and the West African sub-region.
