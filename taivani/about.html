Who We Are

Taivani Consult is a consulting firm of civil and geotechnical engineers based in Accra, Ghana. Since it was founded in 2002, Taivani has maintained a reputation for independent consulting engineering of the highest quality. Today, its staff are committed as ever to providing the same high level of care to all clients and projects.

Geotechnical Engineering is an important part of our operations because at Taivani, we believe a thorough understanding of geotechnical issues is fundamental to the success of most civil, structural, transportation and environmental projects. Each assignment undertaken by Taivani is controlled by the Project Director/Chief Executive Officer who is also directly responsible to the client.

Staff are appointed to a project team on the basis of skills and experience. Within this framework, quality assurance procedures are strictly followed. Thus, our clients have the benefits of easy communication with the project team combined with effective management and control. Taivani provides clients with affordable and sustainable solutions, appropriate to their needs. We achieve this through talented and motivated staff, all showing a passion for professional excellence and high standards of care and service.

We have an excellent reputation for innovation, quality and service. Whatever discipline we work in, the quality that sets Taivani Consult apart is creativity. Creativity is the source of better solutions for our clients and enables us to continue to change and grow in response to rapidly evolving markets.

Taivani project teams have proved that an approach based on a combination of the latest technology, flexibility, imagination and our staff's depth of experience leads to the development of optimum responses to client needs.

Taivani utilizes in-house expertise and knowledge to provide services in the following principal consultancy fields:

  • Civil Engineering
  • Structural Engineering
  • Highway Engineering
  • Project Management and Site Supervision
  • Geotechnical Engineering
  • Environmental Engineering
  • Transportation Planning
  • Geodetic Engineering
  • Rural Water Supply and Sanitation
  • QA/QC and Geotechnical Engineering Laboratory Services

Taivani associates with Baseline Development Solutions for all surveying services, and on large projects, Taivani extends its capabilities through professional affiliations with specialists in order to provide a state-of-the-art approach to projects.
