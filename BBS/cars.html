Leasing, Contract Hire or Lease Purchase?

Choice is a wonderful thing and at BBS Logistics, you can not only select from any current make or model of car available, you can also build a finance package that's completely tailored to you. We provide vehicle leasing, vehicle lease purchase and vehicle contract hire finance options ...but what does each option actually involve and what's the best choice for you?

Leasing

Flexibility is king when it comes to car leasing. You can drive away with a new vehicle with an initial deposit and a monthly payment that works best for you. What happens at the end of the lease though? You can give the car back to us, purchase it or continue to lease it - the choice is yours.

Lease Purchase

When it comes to vehicle lease purchase the clues in the name! You lease the car for a low-cost monthly payment now and still own your car at the end of the agreement. Car lease purchase offers unrivalled flexibility; there are no mileage restrictions on the vehicle and you can even reduce your monthly outgoings by agreeing to a purchase at the start of the lease. If it's flexibility you're after, then vehicle lease purchase is a great option.

Contract Hire

This works best for those who need vehicles to run their day-to-day business activities with minimal stress. Think of Vehicle Contract Hire as something akin to a great value package holiday. We don't just give you the vehicle, you can bundle together a number of services such as maintenance, roadside assistance, vehicle servicing and much more. The best thing is, we package everything into a fixed monthly payment that suits you or your business.
